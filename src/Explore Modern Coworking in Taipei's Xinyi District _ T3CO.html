Check out our amazing space

T3CO is a modern coworking space designed to help you do your best work.

Location

Right in the heart of downtown Taipei in Xinyi district, only 5 minutes walk from City Hall MRT.

Elevate Your Work at T3CO

At T3CO, we’ve designed our space with your productivity and comfort in mind. Here’s a look at the unique features that set our coworking space apart, ensuring you can focus fully on your work in an environment that caters to all your needs:

Kitchen & Dining

Equipped with a lounge/kitchen with complementary coffee and a cozy dining area, T3CO allows you to prepare and savor your meals without ever having to step out. Whether you’re fueling up for the day or winding down, our facilities make it easy and convenient.

Showers and Storage

Start your day fresh or switch gears effortlessly with our on-site showers and personal lockers—perfect for those who bike to work, hit the gym during lunch, or simply need to freshen up after a long day.

Electric Height Adjustable Desks

Adjust your workspace to fit your comfort with our electric height adjustable desks. Whether you prefer standing or sitting while you work, these desks adapt to your preferred style, promoting better posture and well-being.

24/7 Access

Night owl or early riser, T3CO supports your unique workflow by being accessible 24/7. Work on your schedule without the constraints of traditional office hours in a secure and welcoming environment.

Global Community

Join an inspiring community of professionals from around the globe. At T3CO, networking happens naturally. Expand your professional network, share ideas, and collaborate with like-minded individuals from diverse industries.

Events & Workshops

Stay ahead of the curve with our regular events and workshops designed to enhance your skills and knowledge. From tech talks to creative workshops, there’s always an opportunity to learn something new and connect with other professionals.
